1. Introduction

Welcome to our website. The protection of your personal data is important to us. This privacy policy informs you about the data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data. The use of our website is generally possible without providing personal data. If personal data is collected on our pages, it is done voluntarily whenever possible.

2. Data Collection and Processing

We collect and process personal data only to the extent necessary for providing a functional website, including its content and services. Personal data refers to all information that relates to an identified or identifiable natural person.

3. Data We Collect

a. Server Logfiles: Every time you access our website, information is automatically collected that your browser sends to us. This includes:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of access
  • File name and URL requested
  • Website from which the access originated (referrer URL)
  • Used browser and, if applicable, the operating system of your computer, as well as the name of your access provider

b. Contact Form: If you send us inquiries via the contact form, the information you provide, including the contact details you specify, will be stored for the purpose of processing the request and for follow-up inquiries.

c. Registration: If you register on our website, we collect the following data:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password

4. Using Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our website
  • To process your requests and registrations
  • To fulfill our contractual obligations
  • To analyze and improve the effectiveness of our website

5. Sharing Your Data with Third Parties

We will only share your personal data with third parties if this is necessary for the performance of the contract, or if you have explicitly consented to it. Any further sharing of your data will only occur if we are legally obligated to do so.

6. Using Google Ads

Our website uses Google Ads, a service for displaying advertisements. Google Ads uses cookies that enable an analysis of your use of the website. The information generated by the cookies about your use of this website is usually transmitted to a Google server in the US and stored there. Google will use this information to evaluate your use of the website, compile reports on website activity for website operators, and provide other services related to website and internet usage.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about the data we have stored about you
  • Request the correction of incorrect personal data
  • Request the deletion of your personal data stored with us, provided that there is no legal obligation to retain it
  • Request the restriction of the processing of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data
  • Request data portability

8. Data Storage Duration

We will store your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which they were collected, or as required by law.

9. Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect the data we manage from accidental or intentional manipulation, loss, destruction, or unauthorized access by third parties. Our security measures are continuously improved in line with technological developments.

10. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. The current version is always available on our website.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact us. We are happy to assist you.

This privacy policy was created to comply with legal requirements and to provide you with a transparent overview of how your data is processed on our website. We thank you for your trust and assure you that we treat your data with the utmost care and responsibility.
