Standard Recording

Perfect for demo recordings and single tracks. Our standard recording plan offers 2 hours of professional recording time in our acoustically optimized studios. You will receive a basic mix of your recordings, capturing the characteristic sound and atmosphere of your music.

Professional Production

This plan is ideal for EPs and smaller albums. It includes 5 hours of recording time, an advanced mix, and professional mastering to ensure your music comes out in the best possible quality. Our sound engineers are there for you throughout the entire process to ensure every track sounds perfect.

Custom Music Production

Our custom music production offers everything you need for comprehensive projects. This plan includes 10 hours of recording time, detailed mixing and mastering, as well as personal consultation for music arrangement. We help you bring your creative vision to life and present your music in its best form.

VIP Package

For demanding projects and individual requirements, we offer our VIP package. This package provides tailored solutions, including unlimited recording time and full support from our experienced experts. Contact us for a custom offer tailored exactly to your specific needs.
